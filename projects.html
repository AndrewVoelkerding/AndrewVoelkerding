Formula SAE Welded Front Rockers

Purdue Formula SAE Suspension Component

Siemens NX ANSYS FEA GD&T CNC Machining

Designed welded front suspension rockers achieving ~18.8% weight reduction while maintaining a 1.6 Factor of Safety.

  • Modeled assembly components in Siemens NX and performed FEA simulations in ANSYS Mechanical to locate stress concentrations.
  • Generated CAM toolpaths and technical drawings with strict GD&T tolerances to streamline CNC manufacturing.
  • Manufactured multi-operation wheel hubs and custom soft-jaw fixtures using 3-axis CNC milling and hand-tapping.
